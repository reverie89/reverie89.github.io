2026 MR Hongbao Campaign

Year: 2026  |  corporate

This was a partnership with the 2026 Museum Roundtable (MR) Lunar New Year Hongbao Campaign to launch a treasure-hunting type of game titled Hongbao Hunt lah! It’s a super shiok gamified digital experience for everyone to play at six (6) participating museums and heritage institutions to collect Lunar New Year ang baos one. The event ran for one (1) month from 23 January 2026 to 15 February 2026, very happening timing! Participants log into the web game using their Singpass, scan the artefact by solving a riddle and collect their prize at the designated vending machines located in the museums. Public feedback was very positive and the ang baos at several museums were mostly redeemed by the first weekend - so popular until finished so fast!
